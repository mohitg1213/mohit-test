Website Quotation Images

Show product images on Odoo quotations and sales orders!

Odoo Website Quotation Images allow adding images in the quotations. The Odoo app shows product images in quotation descriptions and on printed quotations and sales orders. The Odoo admin must enable an option to display images on the report while creating quotations.

Why Do You Need Website Quotation Images?

By default, Odoo doesn't offer the option to add images on quotations/sales orders. But adding images on both can help boost your sales. It validates the product and functions as a promotional tool for your brand and business.
Website Quotation Images offer image-adding functionality to your Odoo. It allows you to show product images on the order lines of quotations and sales orders. You must enable the quotation images module while creating a quotation from the Odoo backend. Also, this feature shows images in the quotation while quotation to your website users.
Benefits of Website Quotation Images
  • Product images influence customers to make a buying decision quickly.
  • Give customers confidence in products' legitimacy and authenticity.
  • Order quotations and sales orders contain product images.
  • The sent quotation or sales order also contains product images that customers can use for future reference.
